--- layout: client title: Bespoke dressmaking for ball image: ball1.jpg nextpic: ball2 counter: 1 / 4 --- Bolero for Carolynne Ball
modelled by client

Carolynne bought her wedding dress from a bridal shop along with some extra matching silk in order to have a matching bolero made.

The bolero is made in ivory silk with a shaped collar coming up to the neck at the back and cut away over the front. The sleeves are three quarter length. All the edges are finished with piping for a crisp, clean line.

Guide Price: £280
Basic making (10 hours) £220
The piped edge takes at least 3 hours, without a piped edge the cost would be less.
Carolynne sourced her own materials but for something similar budget about £85-£110
