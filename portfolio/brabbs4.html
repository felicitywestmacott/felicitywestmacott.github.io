--- layout: client title: Wedding dress and hand embroidery for Karen Williams image: brabbs4.jpg nextpic: brabbs5 prevpic: brabbs3 counter: 4 / 19 --- Simple modern wedding dress in ivory silk dupion with contrast purple silk detail. Hand embroidered butterflies in silk thread and lace appliqué complete the picture.
Original design sketch. The placement of the the butterflies and lace appliqué was done at a fitting with the bride there to help. We cut out and coloured lots of butterfly designs and pinned them to the dress to decide a balanced but natural formation.
