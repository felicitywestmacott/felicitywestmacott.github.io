Emma commissioned me to make two dresses for her nieces to wear as bridesmaids.

The dresses are in a classic modern style. The bodices are made from ivory duchess-satin with a wide boat neckline and concealed zip fastening at the back. The skirts are made from three layers, soft ivory tulle on top, supported by a layer of ivory dress net and lined with a plain ivory lining to prevent little girls getting 'scratchy' legs. The dresses were finished with coffee coloured sashes in simple satin ribbon.
