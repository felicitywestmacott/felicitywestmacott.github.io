Bridesmaids Dresses for Alice Easton
Beaded sash detail

The sash ends of both dresses were beaded with swarovski crystal diamante stones to echo the beading on Alice's wedding dress.

This hand beading took 4.5 hours per dress (£106) and used £50 of Swarovski stones
