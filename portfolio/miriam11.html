The dress before we began, inside detail

In the 'olden days' (before the 1970's in the case of this phenomenon) most dresses, skirts etc including high end wedding and occasion dresses were not lined as most people wore a slip as a matter of course. It does make the inside of vintage clothing look very different from what we'd expect now from a dress that cost a lot of money.

Miriam chose not to have the a new lining added, though that can be done for most vintage gowns. Nowadays we don't expect to wear dresses with a slip, or to be able to see the 'workings' on the inside.
