Official Wedding pictures by Sebastian Lomas

Martin wanted a coat that would match in with the historical inspired theme of Eleanor's wedding dress.

The coat was made from deep blue silk dupion and lined with a gold and blue shot fabric. We used the groomsmens' hired coats as the basis for his design. The shape is vaguely Victorian, a traditional frock coat style but we did not stick too closely to period style so that Martin felt comfortable.
