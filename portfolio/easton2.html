Official Wedding pictures by Laurence Photography

The dress was created in 6 layers of silk crinkle chiffon over a silk satin base. Netting held out the shape of the skirt.
Each chiffon layer was cut shorter than the one beneath creating graduated petals on the skirt. The edge of each chiffon layer was finished with a roll hem that made them curl and frill a little to emphasise the layering.
