Modelled by Marie

Marie came back for more after having her wedding dress made in 2009. She commissioned a dress to wear to a winter wedding and that would be useful for Christmas parties as well.
The slim fitted dress was lightly boned and made in black silk dupion and shot red/black silk chiffon. The dress had a square neckline with shaped straps which came up over a bust panel of ruched chiffon. The chiffon continued round to the back of the dress where it hung in a graceful cowl shape. Beaded black lace was used to soften the line where the chiffon met the main dress on the underbust.
