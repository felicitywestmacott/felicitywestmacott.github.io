view of finished bolero on stand

The bolero is made is ivory silk with a shaped collar coming up to the neck at the back and cut away over the front. The sleeves are three quarter length. All the edges are finished with piping for a crisp, clean line.

Guide Price: £280
Basic making (10 hours) £220
The piped edge takes at least 3 hours, without a piped edge the cost would be less.
Carolynne sourced her own materials but for something similar budget about £85-£110
